About me

For over a decade, I‘ve partnered with Fortune 500 industry leaders, scrappy start-ups, and mission-driven non-profits to build digital products for screens of all sizes. Before joining GitHub in 2015, I led design teams at Happy Cog and Empathy Lab.

Additionally, I have created a number of personal projects to push my own limits, share what I know, or fill a void in my own processes.
