I design and build for the web with the team at GitHub.

I’ve been passionate about creating and designing content for the web since the day my parents came home with a computer that could connect to the internet. In high school, my friends and I setup a small web design shop to make extra cash building websites for local businesses. After attending college at Drexel University in Philadelphia, I cut my teeth at a small startup in New York City before persuing music. After a few years of trying to make it in a band (and being broke), I returned to tech, working for digital agencies in Philadelphia. During this time, I worked with clients like The American Red Cross, DirecTV, Google, Weil Gotshal & Manges, Trek Bicycles, W.L. Gore & Associates, Philly.com, and The US Open. Almost a decade later, I joined the friendly folks at GitHub to help shape the future of software development.
